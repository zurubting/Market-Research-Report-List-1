<p><p>The single-phase electric power meters market comprises two main types: digital and analog electric power meters. Digital electric power meters utilize electronic circuitry to provide precise measurements, often featuring advanced functionalities like data logging and remote monitoring capabilities. In contrast, analog electric power meters use mechanical components, such as dials and gears, to display usage. While digital meters are favored for their accuracy and additional features, analog meters remain in use due to their simplicity and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
