<p><p>The fiber optic interconnects market consists of various connector types, each serving specific applications. ST connectors, known for their bayonet coupling, are widely used in multimode fiber environments. FC connectors, with a threaded mechanism, offer high precision for single-mode applications. LC connectors are compact, enabling high-density installations. MT-RJ connectors facilitate easy duplex connections, while SC connectors are known for their reliable performance and versatility. MU connectors are smaller, designed for high-density requirements, particularly in telecommunications.</p></p>
