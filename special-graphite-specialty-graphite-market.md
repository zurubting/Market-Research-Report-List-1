<p><p>The specialty graphite market encompasses various types, each serving unique applications. Isotropic graphite features uniform properties in all directions, ideal for high-performance applications like electrodes. Extruded graphite is produced through a molding process, exhibiting high density and strength, suitable for components in high-temperature environments. Molded graphite, shaped under high pressure, offers excellent thermal and electrical conductivity, used in industries like aerospace and electronics. Other types include flexible graphite and natural graphite, which cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
