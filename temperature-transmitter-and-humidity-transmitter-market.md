<p><p>The temperature and humidity transmitter market includes various types, such as Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs), thermocouples, universal transmitters, and multichannel transmitters. RTDs provide accurate temperature readings through resistance changes, while thermocouples detect temperature using voltage differences from junctions. Universal transmitters can handle multiple types of inputs, making them versatile for different applications. Multichannel transmitters monitor and transmit data from multiple sensors simultaneously, enhancing efficiency in complex systems, which is essential for industries requiring precise environmental control.</p></p>
