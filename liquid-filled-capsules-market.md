<p><p>The liquid-filled capsules market includes two primary types: liquid fill hard capsules and liquid fill soft capsules. Liquid fill hard capsules feature a rigid outer shell that encloses a liquid formulation, providing stability and protection. These are often used to deliver medications that require precise dosing. In contrast, liquid fill soft capsules utilize a flexible, gelatinous shell that enhances bioavailability and is easier to swallow. Both types cater to diverse pharmaceutical and nutraceutical applications, addressing varying consumer needs.</p></p>
