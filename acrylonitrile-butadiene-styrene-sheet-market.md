<p><p>The Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) sheet market includes two primary types: Standard and Flame Retardant. Standard ABS sheets are widely used for their durability, impact resistance, and ease of fabrication, making them ideal for automotive, consumer goods, and electronics. Flame Retardant ABS sheets are chemically treated to resist ignition and slow fire spread, catering to industries with strict safety regulations like construction, transportation, and electrical enclosures.
