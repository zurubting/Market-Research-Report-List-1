<p><p>The High Voltage Supply Cable market can be divided into two main types: Single Mode and Multi-Mode. Single Mode cables are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single optical fiber to transmit signals, which minimizes loss and allows for higher bandwidth. Conversely, Multi-Mode cables use multiple fibers to transmit data over shorter distances, offering advantages in terms of cost and ease of installation. Both types serve distinct applications in power distribution and telecommunications, catering to varying performance and structural needs.</p></p>
