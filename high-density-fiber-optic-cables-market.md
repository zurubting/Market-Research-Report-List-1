<p><strong>The High Density Fiber Optic Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Singlemode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-density fiber optic cables market is primarily divided into two types: singlemode and multimode fibers. Singlemode fiber uses a single light path, enabling long-distance data transmission with minimal signal loss, making it ideal for telecommunications and long-haul networks. Multimode fiber, on the other hand, supports multiple light paths, allowing for shorter distance data transfer and higher bandwidth in confined spaces, commonly used in local area networks (LANs) and data centers. Each type caters to specific communication needs and applications.</p></p>
