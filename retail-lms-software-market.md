<p><p>Retail LMS (Learning Management System) software is available in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based LMS solutions offer flexibility and scalability, allowing retailers to access training resources and manage learning from any location with internet connectivity. This model typically involves subscription pricing. In contrast, on-premise LMS requires installation on a retailer's local servers, providing greater control over data and security but demanding higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to diverse organizational needs and preferences in retail training.</p></p>
