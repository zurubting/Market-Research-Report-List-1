<p><ul><li>Single Strand</li><li>Multiple Strands</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper electric wire market is primarily divided into two types: single strand and multiple strands. Single strand wire consists of a single solid copper conductor, offering high conductivity and minimal resistance, making it ideal for applications requiring durability and high power. In contrast, multiple strands comprise several intertwined copper wires, providing flexibility and enhanced resistance to bending and breaking, which is essential for installations in tight spaces or environments subject to movement. Both types serve distinct applications in electrical systems.</p></p>
