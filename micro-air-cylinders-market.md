<p><strong>The Micro Air Cylinders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting</li><li>Double Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro air cylinders market is categorized into single acting and double acting types. Single acting cylinders utilize air pressure for movement in one direction, while a spring returns the actuator to its original position, making them ideal for applications where space and simplicity are priorities. In contrast, double acting cylinders use air pressure for both forward and reverse strokes, providing greater force and control, making them suitable for more complex applications that require precise movement in both directions.</p></p>
