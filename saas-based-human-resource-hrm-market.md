<p><p>The SaaS-based Human Resource Management (HRM) market can be divided into two primary types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise HRM involves software installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater customization and control but requiring significant IT resources and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based HRM solutions are hosted remotely, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from any device with internet connectivity. This model reduces IT overhead while enabling more rapid updates and integration with other cloud services, making it increasingly popular among businesses.</p></p>
