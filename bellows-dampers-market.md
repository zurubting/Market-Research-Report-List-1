<p><p>The bellows dampers market is primarily segmented into two types: PTFE-type and metal-type dampers. PTFE-type dampers utilize polytetrafluoroethylene, offering excellent chemical resistance and low friction, making them ideal for versatile applications in various industries. In contrast, metal-type dampers are characterized by their durability and ability to withstand higher pressures and temperatures, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types play essential roles in controlling vibrations and dampening noise across sectors such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial machinery.</p></p>
