<p><strong>The Automatic PET Bottle Blowing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stretch Blowing Machines</li><li>Extrusion Blowing Machines</li><li>Injection Blowing Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic PET Bottle Blowing Machine market includes three primary types: Stretch Blowing Machines, Extrusion Blowing Machines, and Injection Blowing Machines. Stretch Blowing Machines use preforms and blow them into bottles through a stretching process, ideal for high-production environments. Extrusion Blowing Machines create bottles by extruding molten plastic into molds, suitable for more complex shapes. Injection Blowing Machines combine injection molding and blowing processes to produce precise, high-quality bottles with intricate designs, often used for smaller runs or specialized applications.</p></p>
