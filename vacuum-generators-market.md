<p><p>The vacuum generators market is primarily categorized into single-stage and multi-stage vacuum generators. In contrast, multi-stage vacuum generators utilize multiple chambers to achieve higher vacuum levels and improved efficiency, making them suitable for more complex applications requiring greater suction power and stability. This differentiation addresses varying industrial and commercial vacuum needs.</p></p>
