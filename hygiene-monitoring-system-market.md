<p><p>The Hygiene Monitoring System market comprises three main types: Instruments, ATP Reagents, and Others. Instruments include devices like ATP testers and swabs that measure cleanliness levels on surfaces. ATP Reagents are chemical components used in tests to detect adenosine triphosphate (ATP), indicating biological contamination. 'Others' encompasses supplementary products and services like software solutions, training, and maintenance tools that enhance the effectiveness of hygiene monitoring. Together, these components ensure consistent hygiene standards across various industries.</p></p>
