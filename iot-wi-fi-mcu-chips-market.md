<p><p>The IoT Wi-Fi MCU chips market is primarily segmented into single-band and dual-band types. Single-band chips operate on a single frequency, typically 2.4 GHz, making them cost-effective for basic applications with lower data rates. In contrast, dual-band chips support both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, allowing for higher data throughput and reduced interference, ideal for more demanding applications. This segmentation caters to varying requirements in connectivity, performance, and cost within IoT environments, influencing device design and functionality.</p></p>
