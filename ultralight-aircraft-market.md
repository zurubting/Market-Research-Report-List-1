<p><p>The ultralight aircraft market comprises three main types: fixed wing, flex wing, and rotary wing. Fixed wing aircraft feature a traditional design with wings and are popular for their stability and speed. Flex wing aircraft, also known as trikes, utilize a flexible wing structure that allows for a lightweight design and easy maneuverability. Rotary wing aircraft, such as gyrocopters, employ rotor blades for lift and can operate in smaller spaces, offering versatility for various applications in recreational and light transport sectors.</p></p>
