Bulletproof Laminated Glass is specifically designed to withstand ballistic impacts, making it ideal for security applications. Both types utilize low-temperature processing techniques, allowing for better adhesion and clarity while providing strengthened structural integrity for diverse architectural and automotive uses.</p></p>
