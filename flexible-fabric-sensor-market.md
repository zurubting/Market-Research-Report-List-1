<p><p>The flexible fabric sensor market comprises several types, each utilizing distinct technologies. Flexible capacitive sensors detect changes in capacitance due to pressure or bending. Flexible piezoresistive sensors measure resistance changes under stress, ideal for wearables. Flexible piezo sensors generate electrical signals upon deformation, useful in motion detection. Flexible inductive sensors respond to changes in inductance caused by movement. Flexible fiber optic sensors leverage light transmission to detect strain and temperature variations, offering high sensitivity and durability in various applications.</p></p>
