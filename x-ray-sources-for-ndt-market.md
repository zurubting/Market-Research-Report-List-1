<p><strong>The X-Ray Sources for NDT Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-tube</li><li>Sealed-tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) market, X-ray sources are categorized into open-tube and sealed-tube types. Open-tube X-ray sources utilize a vacuum tube where electrons collide with a target to produce X-rays, offering high flexibility and customizable energy levels for specific applications. Sealed-tube sources, on the other hand, consist of a pre-packaged, sealed unit that provides reliable and consistent X-ray output, making them ideal for portable and automated systems. Both types serve critical roles in quality control and material inspection processes.</p></p>
