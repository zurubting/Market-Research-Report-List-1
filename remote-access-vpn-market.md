<p><strong>The Remote-Access VPN Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SSL VPN</li><li>IPSec VPN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The remote-access VPN market primarily consists of two types: SSL VPN and IPSec VPN. SSL VPN utilizes Secure Sockets Layer technology, enabling secure access to applications via web browsers, making it user-friendly and ideal for remote workers needing flexibility. In contrast, IPSec VPN provides a robust encryption protocol for establishing secure connections over the internet, often used for site-to-site communications and ensuring data integrity. Both types cater to distinct security needs in the evolving digital landscape.</p></p>
