<p><strong>The Medical Image Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Medical Image Management System</li><li>Vendor Neutral Archive</li><li>Application-Independent Clinical Archive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical image management market encompasses several key types. Medical Image Management Systems (MIMS) optimize image handling, storage, and retrieval for efficient diagnostics. Vendor Neutral Archives (VNA) enable healthcare providers to store images from multiple sources in a standardized format, ensuring interoperability. Application-Independent Clinical Archives (AICA) allow for the storage of clinical data, including images, independently of specific applications, facilitating data access and analytics across various healthcare platforms. Together, these systems enhance patient care through better image accessibility and management.</p></p>
