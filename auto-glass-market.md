<p><p>The auto glass market consists of several types, primarily including laminated glass, tempered glass, and others. Laminated glass, known for its safety features, consists of two glass layers bonded with a plastic interlayer, making it shatter-resistant. Tempered glass, subjected to heating and rapid cooling, offers enhanced strength and thermal resistance, often used in side and rear windows. Other types include polycarbonate and specialty glasses, providing unique benefits for specific applications in vehicles, contributing to the market's diverse offerings.</p></p>
