<p><p>The flexible solar cell market features various technologies, notably Copper Indium Gallium Selenide (CIGS) and Amorphous Silicon (a-Si). CIGS cells are known for their high efficiency and ability to be applied to a variety of surfaces, making them ideal for niche applications, such as building-integrated photovoltaics. In contrast, a-Si cells are lightweight, low-cost, and perform well in low-light conditions, though they typically offer lower efficiency. Both technologies are key players in the growing demand for versatile and adaptable solar solutions.</p></p>
