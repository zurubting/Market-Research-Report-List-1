<p><p>The solar photovoltaic (PV) panels market comprises several types. Mono-Si modules, known for high efficiency, are made from single-crystal silicon. Multi-Si modules use multiple silicon crystals, offering a balance between cost and efficiency. CdTe modules utilize cadmium telluride for thin-film technology, providing flexibility and lighter weight. CIGS modules combine copper, indium, gallium, and selenium, known for their lightweight and high efficiency. A-Si modules are amorphous silicon thin films, suitable for low-light conditions. Other modules may include innovative technologies like perovskite and organic photovoltaics.</p></p>
