<p><p>The Pasteur pipette tip market encompasses two main types: non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are designed for general laboratory applications, allowing for direct sample transfer without any filtration. In contrast, filtered pipette tips include a built-in barrier that prevents liquid contamination and aerosol formation, protecting both samples and the pipette. This differentiation makes filtered tips particularly valuable in sensitive procedures, such as in molecular biology and microbiology, where sample integrity is crucial.</p></p>
