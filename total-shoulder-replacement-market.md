<p><strong>The Total Shoulder Replacement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anatomic Total Shoulder Replacements</li><li>Reverse Total Shoulder Replacements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The total shoulder replacement market primarily includes two types: Anatomic and Reverse total shoulder replacements. Anatomic total shoulder replacements involve replacing the damaged humeral head and glenoid with metal components, closely mimicking the natural anatomy and functioning well in patients with intact rotator cuffs. In contrast, Reverse total shoulder replacements swap the positions of the ball and socket, providing improved stability and function, especially for patients with severely damaged rotator cuffs or complex shoulder issues, enhancing their quality of life.</p></p>
