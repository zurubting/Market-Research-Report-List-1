<p><p>The building thermal insulation market comprises various materials, each with unique properties. Glass wool is lightweight and offers excellent thermal performance, often used in residential and commercial buildings. Mineral wool is renowned for its fire resistance and sound insulation, suitable for industrial applications. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is a cost-effective option, providing good insulation and moisture resistance. Extruded Polystyrene (XPS) delivers superior insulation in lower thicknesses and is ideal for below-grade applications. These materials contribute significantly to energy efficiency in buildings.</p></p>
