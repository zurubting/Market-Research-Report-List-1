<p><p>Thermopile and infrared temperature sensors are critical devices used for non-contact temperature measurement. Thermopiles consist of multiple thermocouples connected in series, generating a voltage proportional to temperature differences. Infrared sensors capture and measure the infrared radiation emitted by objects, allowing for precise temperature readings without direct contact. These sensors are widely used in applications ranging from industrial processes and medical devices to automotive and consumer electronics.</p><p>The Thermopile and Infrared Temperature Sensors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for advanced temperature measurement solutions in various industries. Through Hole Type sensors are designed for traditional PCB mounting, providing robust connections and durability, often preferred in industrial applications. In contrast, SMD Type sensors are compact, enabling higher circuit density and automated production, making them ideal for consumer electronics. Both types are essential for applications requiring non-contact temperature measurements across various sectors, including automotive, healthcare, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
