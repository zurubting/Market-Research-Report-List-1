<p><p>The Hot Melt Spray Gun market comprises two primary types: pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic spray guns utilize compressed air to atomize and apply hot melt adhesives, offering high speed and efficiency, ideal for large-scale applications. In contrast, electric spray guns operate using an electric heating element, providing more precise control over adhesive application and temperature. Both types cater to various industries, such as packaging, woodworking, and automotive, each chosen based on specific project requirements and operational preferences.</p></p>
