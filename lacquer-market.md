<p><p>The lacquer market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based lacquers utilize organic solvents for a smooth finish and quick drying, providing durability and resistance to chemicals, making them ideal for furniture and automotive applications. In contrast, water-based lacquers are eco-friendly, featuring low volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and easier clean-up, suitable for indoor use and promoting better air quality. Each type caters to different preferences and regulatory requirements, influencing market trends and consumer choices.</p></p>
