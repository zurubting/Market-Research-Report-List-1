<p><p>The electromagnetic clutch system market is primarily divided into two types: single disk clutches and multi-disk clutches. Single disk clutches feature a single friction surface and are commonly used in smaller applications where space and weight are concerns. In contrast, multi-disk clutches incorporate multiple friction surfaces, allowing for higher torque transmission and improved performance, making them suitable for heavier and more demanding applications. Both types are essential for efficient power transmission in various automotive and industrial settings.</p></p>
