<p><p>The Oil and Gas Storage and Transportation market encompasses various types of gas and liquid forms for effective distribution. PNG (Piped Natural Gas) is transported via pipelines, while LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) is cooled for shipping in specialized tankers. CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) is stored under high pressure for vehicles. ANG (Adsorbed Natural Gas) uses porous materials for storage, and NGH (Natural Gas Hydrate) involves gas trapped in ice-like structures. Other transport methods may include conventional liquids and alternative fuels.</p></p>
