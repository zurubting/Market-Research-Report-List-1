<p><ul><li>Open Suction Catheter</li><li>Closed Suction Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical suction catheters market is categorized into two main types: open suction catheters and closed suction catheters. Open suction catheters are typically used in procedures where the airway needs to be cleared, allowing for direct access and visual inspection. In contrast, closed suction catheters consist of a system that permits suction without disconnecting the ventilated patient from the ventilator, reducing the risk of contamination and maintaining airway protection, making them essential in critical care settings.</p></p>
