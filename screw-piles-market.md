<p><p>The screw piles market encompasses various types, including SS-RS combinations, which integrate square and round shafts for enhanced load-bearing capacity. SS (Square Shaft) screw piles feature a square profile, providing increased torque and stability, while RS (Round Shaft) screw piles offer flexibility and ease of installation. Helical Grouted Displacement (HGD) piles combine helical design with grouting to improve soil interaction and support heavy structures. Each type caters to different engineering requirements, optimizing performance based on specific project demands.</p></p>
