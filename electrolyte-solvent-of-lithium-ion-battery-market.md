Dimethyl carbonate (DMC) is known for its low viscosity and high dielectric constant, enhancing conductivity. Ethyl methyl carbonate (EMC) offers good stability and performance at high temperatures. Diethyl carbonate (DEC) provides a balance between conductivity and stability. Propylene carbonate (PC) has a high boiling point, useful for high-energy applications. Vinyl carbonate acts as an additive for improved cycling performance, while others include niche solvents for specialized applications.</p></p>
