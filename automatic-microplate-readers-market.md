<p><strong>The Automatic Microplate Readers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li> Optical Filter Microplate Reader</li><li>Optical Grating Microplate Reader </li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic microplate readers are essential instruments in life sciences for analyzing samples in various formats. The optical filter microplate reader utilizes specific filters to isolate wavelengths of light, allowing it to measure absorbance or fluorescence accurately. In contrast, the optical grating microplate reader employs diffraction gratings to separate light into its component wavelengths, enabling more versatile detection of multi-wavelength signals. Both types cater to distinct experimental needs, with filter readers generally preferred for simplicity and grating readers for advanced applications.</p></p>
