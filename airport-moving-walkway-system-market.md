<p><p>The airport moving walkway system market is primarily categorized into two types: belt type and pallet type. Belt type walkways have a continuous belt that moves passengers along, typically used for shorter distances. In contrast, pallet type walkways feature a series of flat pallets that move in a continuous loop, providing a stable surface for passengers, ideal for longer distances. Both types enhance passenger flow and improve travel efficiency within airports, catering to different operational needs and space configurations.</p></p>
