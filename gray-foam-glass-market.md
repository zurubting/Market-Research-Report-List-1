<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gray Foam Glass market is segmented into two main types: closed cell and open cell. Closed cell foam glass is characterized by its distinct, non-porous structure, providing excellent thermal insulation and moisture resistance, making it ideal for applications in construction and refrigeration. In contrast, open cell foam glass has a more porous structure, offering lightweight and sound absorption properties, suitable for applications like acoustic insulation. Both types cater to diverse needs across various industries, emphasizing insulation and lightweight characteristics.</p></p>
