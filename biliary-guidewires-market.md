<p><p>The biliary guidewires market comprises two main types: single-use and reusable guidewires. Single-use guidewires are designed for one-time procedures, ensuring optimal sterility and reducing the risk of cross-contamination. They are convenient for healthcare providers and improve patient safety. In contrast, reusable guidewires can be sterilized and used multiple times, offering cost savings for healthcare facilities over the long term. Each type serves specific clinical needs and preferences, influencing their adoption in various medical settings.</p></p>
