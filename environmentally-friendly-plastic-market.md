<p><p>The environmentally friendly plastic market encompasses three main types. Bio-based non-biodegradable plastics are derived from renewable sources but do not break down naturally, maintaining durability for specific applications. Biobased biodegradable plastics, made from organic materials, decompose effectively under suitable conditions, making them suitable for short-term use. Petroleum-based biodegradable plastics combine traditional petroleum sources with additives that promote decomposition, offering a temporary solution to plastic waste while still relying on fossil fuels. Each type serves different environmental needs and application contexts.</p></p>
