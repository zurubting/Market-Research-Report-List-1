<p><ul><li>Standalone Tracker</li><li>OBD Device</li><li>Advance Tracker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tracking devices market consists of three primary types: standalone trackers, OBD devices, and advanced trackers. Standalone trackers are portable units used for personal tracking, often equipped with GPS. OBD devices plug into a vehicle's onboard diagnostics port, providing real-time data on vehicle performance and location. Advanced trackers combine GPS with IoT technology, enabling features like geofencing, driver behavior monitoring, and enhanced analytics for fleet management. Together, these devices enhance safety, efficiency, and asset management across various applications.</p></p>
