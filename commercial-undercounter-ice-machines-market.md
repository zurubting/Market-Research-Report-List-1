<p><strong>The Commercial Undercounter Ice Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Cooled</li><li>Air Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial undercounter ice machine market primarily features two types: water-cooled and air-cooled units. Water-cooled machines use a water source to dissipate heat, making them efficient for indoor use, particularly in high-demand environments. However, they require a continuous water supply. In contrast, air-cooled machines rely on ambient air for cooling, making them more energy-efficient and easier to set up. These units are ideal for locations with sufficient ventilation. Both types cater to the needs of restaurants, bars, and hospitality venues.</p></p>
