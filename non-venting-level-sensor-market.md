<p><ul><li>Float Type Liquid Level Sensor</li><li>Hydrostatic Liquid Level Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Venting Level Sensor market comprises two main types: Float Type Liquid Level Sensors and Hydrostatic Liquid Level Sensors. Float Type Sensors utilize a buoyant float that rises and falls with liquid levels, providing accurate readings through mechanical or electronic signals. Hydrostatic Liquid Level Sensors, on the other hand, measure the pressure exerted by a liquid column, allowing for precise level monitoring regardless of the fluid's characteristics. Both types are essential in various industrial applications for effective liquid management.</p></p>
