<p><p>Fibreboard for construction is categorized into three types based on density: Low Density Fiberboard (LDF), Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF), and High Density Fiberboard (HDF). LDF is lightweight and ideal for insulation and acoustic applications. MDF offers a balance of strength and flexibility, making it suitable for furniture and cabinetry. HDF is the most robust, providing durability for heavy-duty applications such as flooring and structural panels. Each type caters to specific needs within the construction industry, enhancing design and functionality.</p></p>
