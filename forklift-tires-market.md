<p><p>The forklift tires market is categorized into three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic forklift tires are air-filled, offering enhanced cushioning and traction for outdoor and rough surfaces. Solid forklift tires are made of solid rubber, providing durability and puncture resistance, ideal for indoor use. Polyurethane forklift tires combine lightweight properties with excellent wear resistance, making them suitable for varied surfaces while minimizing floor damage. Each type caters to specific operational needs and environments, ensuring efficiency and safety.</p></p>
